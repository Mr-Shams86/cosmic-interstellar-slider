— The first confirmed interstellar object, discovered by Pan-STARRS in 2017 (designation 1I/2017 U1).

2I/Borisov

— The first known interstellar comet, discovered in 2019 by Gennady Borisov; later imaged by the Hubble Space Telescope.

Learn more

3I/Atlas

— The third interstellar object, discovered on July 1, 2025 by the ATLAS survey; now studied by Hubble and JWST. Early results show an unusually CO₂-rich coma (CO₂/H₂O ≈ 8:1).

Learn more

OK/2019

— A ~100 m asteroid spotted only days in advance; on 25 Jul 2019 it passed safely at ~73,000 km (~0.19 of the Moon’s distance).

Learn more

DZ2/2023

— A ~40–90 m near-Earth asteroid that safely flew past on 25 Mar 2023 at ~175,000 km (~0.45 of the Moon’s distance).
